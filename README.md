## About 

Welcome to **Exodus**! This script is a Semi-AFK script designed to automate quizzes, tests, exams and much more on Edgenuity. It features automatic answering, auto-advancement with customizable delays, auto-submit functionality, AI Answers integration with Auto Writing capablities (includes an AI humanizer), and MUCH more! Visit the [Exodus Website](https://exodusbot.net) for an updated full list of features.

### Features ⭐

See the full, regularly updated list at [exodusbot.net/features](https://exodusbot.net/features). All **30+** features are listed below, grouped by category.

#### 🤖 Automation

- **Auto Move**: Automatically moves to next activity after completion.
- **Auto Advance**: Customize how long Exodus waits before advancing on your current page/frame by entering any number in seconds.
- **Auto Submit**: Automatically submits the Quiz, Test, Exam, Essay, or Language Activity with adjustable delays (second, minute, or ten-minute delay).
- **Auto Answers**: Auto-fills graded assessment activities.
- **Auto Ungraded**: Auto-fills ungraded assessment activities by guessing.
- **Auto Assignment**: Automatically answers and advances on questions labelled as **Assignment, Wet-Lab, and Lab** activities.
- **Auto Vocab**: Automatically completes activities labelled as **"Vocabulary"**.
- **Interactive Frame Skipper**: Automatically skips Math Proofs, Virtual Lab frames, and annoying drag and drop activities.
- **Language Activity Skipper**: Automates language activities efficiently by bypassing them, but waits your Submit Delay before skipping.
- **AI Answers**: Uses AI to provide accurate answers for written text questions like essays and short answers, saving time and improving efficiency.
- **AI eNotes**: Automatically generates AI eNotes for you and saves them on both regular Edgenuity and EdgeEX.
- **Auto Project**: Submits a corrupt PDF on project activities. Customize delay with Auto Submit Delay.

#### ✨ Quality of Life

- **Skip Intros & Outros**: Automatically skips the audio clips at the beginning and end of questions by deleting the invisible overlay, saving time.
- **Show Columns**: Automatically displays all "Sample Response" columns in supported questions, which are usually hidden until after submitting an answer.
- **Discord Logging**: Logs your current activity and sends it via a Discord webhook, showing time, progression, and real time alerts.
- **Frame Unlocker**: Removes restrictions on frames to allow seamless navigation through all questions without waiting for previous ones to be completed.
- **Proctorio Spoofer**: Bypass Proctorio on Edgenuity and EdgeEX exams. A separate Windows app that launches a clean, spoofed Chrome session so Proctorio cannot fingerprint your machine or environment, keeping your exam session completely ordinary to Edgenuity and your school.
- **Brainly Lockpicker**: Built-in Brainly bypass. Search and page unlock work without a Brainly Plus subscription.
- **Progress Bar**: Onscreen progress bar for regular Edgenuity that shows how far you are through your current class.
- **Anti Logout**: Prevents automatic logout due to inactivity.
- **Hide Info**: Conceals personally identifiable information such as your name, course names, Exodus key, and browser tab title for privacy, screenshots, or recording.
- **Hide UI**: Hides the menu by removing it from the page. Press `CTRL-SHIFT-H` to unhide it.
- **Multi-Tabs**: Lifts the single-tab restriction, enabling multiple classes to be open at once for better multitasking.
- **Presets**: Import, export, and save up to five Exodus configs as presets. Share your settings in the #configs channel in our Discord and browse user-made community presets for easy setup.

#### 🧪 EdgeEX

- **Skip Videos (EdgeEX)**: Skip Edgenuity EX videos.
- **Auto Ex Advance (EdgeEX)**: Automatically advances through EdgeEX activities with configurable delays by activity type and time range.
- **Auto Ex Submit (EdgeEX)**: Submits any EdgeEX Instruction / Assignment question or Assessment Activity after its completion, with the same configurable delays as Auto Ex Advance (Activity Type / Time Range).
- **Auto Ex Graded (EdgeEX)**: Automatically answers every EdgeEX question using Exodus AI with 95% accuracy (excluding math), supporting MCQs, dropdowns, non-math text boxes, drag and drop, and more.
- **Auto Ex Ungraded/Assignment (EdgeEX)**: Automatically completes Ungraded and Assignment activities on EdgeEX with 100% accuracy, keeping your grades perfect across all content types.

#### 🎨 Themes

- **Custom Background**: Set a custom background image or GIF via URL.
- **Custom Fonts**: Choose between 10 custom fonts applied across the whole page: Default (Poppins), Lexend, Arial, Comic Sans, Frutiger, Verdana, Courier New, Impact, Times New Roman, Roboto.
- **Dark Mode**: Applies a sleek dark theme across the Edgenuity homepage, activity pages, and more for a less straining experience.
- **Menu Theme**: Switch between 12 themes (Light, Dark, Red, Blue Sky, Forest, Purple, Rose, Mocha, Sunset, Midnight, and Default), each completely overhauling the menu colors and overall look.
